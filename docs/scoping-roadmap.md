# Product Scoping & Roadmap

Core feature: Structured earnings-call signal extraction using guidance shifts, tone-change moments, and transcript evidence.

## Now
- Deterministic pipeline for one earnings call to produce a structured signal summary.
- Guidance extraction, tone-change moments, and transcript-backed evidence snippets.

## Next
- Prior-call guidance comparison to surface clearer change direction.
- Tighter summary prioritization for trader review.

## Later
- Broader cross-call benchmarking and refinement of signal quality.

**Multimodal audio signals**
- speaking-rate change
- pause / hesitation detection
- emphasis / pitch variability
- alignment of vocal shifts with transcript guidance statements
